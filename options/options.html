About & help
What this extension does, and how to reach me.
What it is A summarizer for YouTube. On any video that has captions, it adds a panel that turns the transcript into a clean, timestamped summary — so you can read the gist in a minute or jump straight to the part you care about.
How it works It pulls the video's transcript, sends it to the AI provider you've set up (Google Gemini or Mistral, using your own key), and renders the result as expandable sections — each with a timestamp that seeks the player when you click it.
Detail & theme Dial each summary from a quick skim to a thorough breakdown with the Detail control in the panel header. The panel follows YouTube's light or dark theme on its own — override it any time under Appearance.
Privacy Your API keys and stats live only in your browser's local storage, never on a server of mine. Transcripts are sent only to the provider you choose, and nowhere else.
